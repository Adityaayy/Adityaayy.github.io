Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DoorThing

How does DoorThing work with existing locks? +

DoorThing mounts directly over your existing deadbolt. It has a motorized mechanism that turns your lock when you scan your ID. Your physical key still works normally as a backup.

What types of ID cards are compatible? +

DoorThing works with any RFID-enabled student ID card (125kHz or 13.56MHz). It also supports NFC-enabled smartphones and wearable devices.

How long does the battery last? +

With typical student usage (10-15 unlocks per day), DoorThing lasts 30+ days on a single charge. You'll get notifications on your phone when battery is low.

Is DoorThing secure? +

Absolutely. We use military-grade encryption for all communications. Your ID card data is never stored or transmitted. DoorThing is more secure than traditional keys which can be duplicated.
